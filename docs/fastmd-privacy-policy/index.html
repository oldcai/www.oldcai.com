FastMD Privacy Policy

oldcai
2026-07-12
FastMD

Effective date: 2026-07-12
Last updated: 2026-07-12

English

Summary

FastMD is local by default. It has no account, advertising, analytics, telemetry, remote logging, or background upload. Your Markdown documents remain on your Mac unless you explicitly choose to publish a preview through Cloudflare Drop.

Information stored on your Mac

FastMD stores only app settings needed for its features, including the last window frame and security-scoped bookmarks for folders you explicitly authorize. Document contents and exported files remain in locations you choose. FastMD does not send these settings to the developer.

Optional Cloudflare Drop publishing

When you choose Publish Preview via Cloudflare Drop…, FastMD first asks for confirmation. If you continue, the app sends an HTML snapshot of the current Markdown document and readable local resources explicitly referenced by that document to Cloudflare. Cloudflare creates a temporary, publicly accessible workers.dev link.

This optional transfer is used only to provide the publishing feature. FastMD does not link the uploaded content to an account or use it for tracking, advertising, or analytics. Do not publish confidential, personal, or third-party material unless you have permission to make it public.

Cloudflare may process uploaded content, IP addresses, service logs, cookies, and related technical information under its Privacy Policy and Terms. The developer does not control Cloudflare’s retention or processing. If you do not use this feature, FastMD makes no app-initiated network request.

Quick Look and local files

The bundled Quick Look extension is sandboxed and read-only. It does not have network access. FastMD requests user-selected file or folder access only for opening and saving documents, displaying referenced local resources, showing same-folder files, and following external saves.

Tracking and third-party SDKs

FastMD does not track you and contains no advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Your choices

  • Decline folder access; the document remains usable, while folder-dependent features may be limited.
  • Do not use Cloudflare publishing; no document content is transmitted by FastMD.
  • Remove local settings by uninstalling the app and deleting its app container in macOS.
  • A temporary Cloudflare deployment may expire automatically. FastMD cannot guarantee immediate deletion from Cloudflare after publishing.

Contact

Questions or requests about this policy: oldcai.com@gmail.com

简体中文

概要

FastMD 默认仅在本机工作。应用无需账户，不含广告、分析、遥测、远程日志或后台上传。除非你主动选择通过 Cloudflare Drop 发布预览，否则 Markdown 文档始终留在你的 Mac 上。

保存在 Mac 上的信息

FastMD 只保存功能所需的本机设置，包括最近一次窗口位置，以及你明确授权的文件夹安全作用域书签。文档内容和导出文件保存在你选择的位置。这些设置不会发送给开发者。

可选的 Cloudflare Drop 发布

当你选择 Publish Preview via Cloudflare Drop… 时，FastMD 会先要求确认。继续后，应用会把当前 Markdown 文档的 HTML 快照，以及文档明确引用且可读取的本地资源发送给 Cloudflare。Cloudflare 会生成一个临时、公开可访问的 workers.dev 链接。

该可选传输仅用于提供发布功能。FastMD 不会把上传内容关联到用户账户，也不会用于追踪、广告或分析。请勿发布机密信息、个人信息，或你无权公开的第三方内容。

Cloudflare 可能依据其隐私政策与条款处理上传内容、IP 地址、服务日志、Cookie 和相关技术信息。开发者无法控制 Cloudflare 的保留与处理方式。如果你不使用此功能，FastMD 不会主动发起网络请求。

“快速查看”和本地文件

随附的“快速查看”扩展运行在只读沙箱中，没有网络权限。FastMD 仅为打开与保存文档、显示引用的本地资源、展示同文件夹文件，以及跟随外部保存而请求用户选择的文件或文件夹权限。

追踪与第三方 SDK

FastMD 不会追踪你，也不包含广告、分析或追踪 SDK。

你的选择

  • 你可以拒绝文件夹访问；文档仍可使用，但依赖文件夹的功能可能受限。
  • 你可以不使用 Cloudflare 发布；FastMD 不会传输文档内容。
  • 你可以卸载应用并删除其 macOS App 容器，以移除本机设置。
  • 临时 Cloudflare 部署可能自动过期；发布后，FastMD 无法保证 Cloudflare 会立即删除内容。

联系

有关本政策的问题或请求：oldcai.com@gmail.com
